About

E-ISSN 2225-0530

Proceedings of the Institute of Applied Mathematics is a peer-reviewed journal published two times (semiannual) in a year. The main aim of the journal is to publish refereed, well-written original research articles, and studies that describe the latest research and developments in all areas of Pure and Applied Mathematics. The journal is published in both printed and online versions. The online version is free to access and download. The journal is indexed in Google Scholar, VINITI RAS, Information Matrix for the Analysis of Journals, Research Bible, JournalFactor, JIFACTOR....
